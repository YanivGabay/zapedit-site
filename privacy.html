Zapedit

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Zapedit ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares your information when you use our Chrome extension and related services.

1. Data Collection

We collect the following types of data:

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in with Google OAuth, we collect:

  • Email address - Used to identify your account
  • Name - Used for display purposes
  • Profile picture URL - Used for display purposes (optional)

1.2 Usage Data

  • Credit balance - Number of image edits remaining
  • Transaction history - Records of credit purchases

1.3 Image Data

When you use our image editing features:

  • Images you select for editing are temporarily transmitted to our servers
  • Images are processed by AI and immediately deleted after processing
  • We do NOT store, retain, or archive your images
  • We do NOT use your images to train AI models

1.4 Payment Information

Payment processing is handled entirely by LemonSqueezy. We receive:

  • Confirmation of successful payments
  • Email address associated with the purchase

We do NOT collect or store credit card numbers, billing addresses, or other financial details.

2. Data Handling

Here's how we process your data:

Data Type How We Use It
Email address Account identification, purchase verification, service communications
Credit balance Track remaining image edits, deduct credits after use
Images (temporary) Process with AI to apply requested edits, then immediately delete
Transaction records Credit your account with purchased credits

3. Data Storage

3.1 Where We Store Data

Your account data is stored on:

  • Supabase (our database provider) - Servers located in AWS data centers
  • Local browser storage - Authentication tokens stored locally in your Chrome browser

3.2 Data Retention Periods

Data Type Retention Period
Account information Until you request account deletion
Credit balance Until you request account deletion
Transaction history 7 years (for legal/tax compliance)
Images submitted for editing Deleted immediately after processing (typically under 60 seconds)
Authentication tokens Until you sign out or tokens expire

3.3 Data Security

  • All data transmitted between your browser and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
  • Database access is protected by authentication and access controls
  • We use industry-standard security practices

4. Data Sharing

We share data with the following third-party services:

Service Data Shared Purpose
Google (OAuth) Authentication request Sign in with Google
Google Gemini API Images you edit (temporarily) AI image processing
Supabase Account data, credit balance Database hosting
LemonSqueezy Email address (for purchases) Payment processing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your images for any purpose other than processing your edit request

5. Chrome Extension Permissions

Our extension requests the following permissions:

Permission Why We Need It
contextMenus Add "Edit with Zapedit" option when you right-click images
activeTab Access the current tab to display editing overlay
storage Store your authentication session locally
Host permissions Fetch images from websites for editing

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your personal data
  • Deletion - Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Correction - Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Portability - Request your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent - Stop using our service at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at yaniv242@gmail.com

7. Cookies and Local Storage

Our website may use cookies for analytics. The Chrome extension uses browser local storage to:

  • Store your authentication session
  • Remember your preferences

You can clear this data by signing out or clearing your browser data.

8. Children's Privacy

Zapedit is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting a notice on our website
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
