Common Questions

The extension isn't working on a website

Some websites block extensions. Try refreshing the page, or make sure you're right-clicking directly on an image element. The extension doesn't work on browser internal pages (chrome://, about:, etc).

My edit failed or looks wrong

AI isn't perfect. Try rephrasing your custom prompt, or use a different preset. If an edit fails, your credit is automatically refunded.

How do I get more credits?

Click on the Zapedit extension icon in your browser to see your remaining credits and purchase more.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, contact us within 14 days of purchase if you're not satisfied and we'll refund your unused credits.

Is my data private?

Yes. Images are processed securely and not stored after editing. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
