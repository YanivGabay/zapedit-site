FAQ

What is a credit?

Each image edit costs 1 credit. Credits never expire and can be used anytime.

How does it work?

Zapedit is a Chrome extension. After installing, right-click any image on the web to edit it with AI. The edited image appears right on the page for you to download.

What AI model do you use?

We use Google's Gemini AI for image understanding and editing, providing fast and high-quality results.

Is my data safe?

Images are processed securely and not stored after editing. We only keep minimal account data needed to track your credits. See our Privacy Policy for details.
